This presentation was created as a response to seeing some common things that we didn't like in lots of engineering presentations. By no means is this a complete guide, nor are our opinions really anything but our own opinions. Take it mostly as "things that our advisor said to not do".

Please feel free to spread this around and give us feedback via email.

One slide in the presentation was adapted from a slide in a presentation given on November 6, 2003, at the Inagural Workshop of the National Intelligence Council 2020 Project sponsored by the CIA. The presentation by Theodore Gordon is available via the CIA's NIC 2020 Web Site. I found this slide via a Google search and liked the colors and Venn Diagram. I have not contacted Thodore Gordon regarding permission to use the adapted slide, as no contact information was given.
